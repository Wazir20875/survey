Welcome to the NHS Staff Survey Insights Platform. This platform provides in-depth results and analysis from the 2024 NHS Staff Survey, focusing on the most important factors that contribute to a safe, supportive, and healthy working environment for NHS staff.

We go beyond the standard survey summaries to bring you powerful visual dashboards and interactive tools that highlight how NHS organisations across England are performing on key themes such as Health and Wellbeing, Bullying and Harassment, Violence, Burnout, and Flexible Working.
